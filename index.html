How It Works

Our suggestion box system is designed to gather valuable feedback and suggestions from you. Here's how it works:

  1. Explore the Navigation Bar: Use the navigation bar at the top to select the appropriate category for your suggestion. We have sections for administrators, prefects, teachers, chaplaincy, non-teaching staff, form period, and students.
  2. Fill Out the Form: Once you've selected a category, you'll be presented with a form. Fill in your suggestion in the provided field, and include your name for identification.
  3. Submit Your Suggestion: Click the "Submit" button to send us your suggestion. Your input is important to us, and we'll review it carefully.
  4. Re-visit: You can always come back to this page to check for updates on your suggestion or to submit more feedback.
